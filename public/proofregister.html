Proof of Funds Availability

Proof of funds (PoF) is a verification process that confirms an individual or entity has the required assets or liquidity to complete a transaction. In traditional finance, this might involve bank statements or other financial records. In the crypto space, this verification is usually achieved through blockchain technology, where digital signatures and addresses are used to confirm the presence of sufficient funds in a wallet before a transaction is processed.

02

Why is this Important?

Ensure Transaction Reliability:

Proof of funds checks add a layer of security to ensure that all participants in a transaction can meet their obligations, preventing fraud and enhancing trust in financial interactions.
